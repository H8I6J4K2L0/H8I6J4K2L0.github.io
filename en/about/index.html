Hello there! I’m a self-taught painter driven by an unwavering passion for art that has been with me since my earliest memories. While I hold a degree in English Philology from the University of Malaga, my professional journey has led me to become a dedicated high school teacher. Yet, beyond my teaching role, my heart truly belongs to the world of art.

Nurturing the Flame

Art has been woven into the very fabric of my existence ever since I was a child. My fascination with artistic expression transformed into a dedicated hobby that has persisted through the years. I embarked on a journey of self-education, honing my skills and expanding my artistic horizons. Additionally, I had the privilege of learning from esteemed professors specializing in Fine Arts at various distinguished artistic institutions within my city.

A Proud Affiliation

As a proud member of the Malaga Association of Visual Artists (APLAMA), I draw immense inspiration from the vibrant and dynamic artistic community that surrounds me. This affiliation serves as a constant wellspring of creativity, propelling me to explore new dimensions in my work. I firmly believe that art possesses the extraordinary ability to channel emotions, narrate intricate stories, and ignite profound contemplation.

Exploring My Creations

I extend a warm invitation to explore my portfolio showcased in the “Exhibition” section. Each piece is a labor of love, a result of countless hours of dedication and unbridled imagination. Should you wish to connect or inquire about my artistry, please don’t hesitate to reach out via the “Contact” page. Your interest and support mean the world to me, and I’m truly grateful for the opportunity to share my artistic odyssey with you.

Looking ahead with anticipation to the wonderful journey of sharing my creations with fellow art enthusiasts!
